    Network of business

    Here is a quick animation where you can visualize the network of businesses as described above.You can play with two parameters: the year of the elite users that link the businesses and the possibility, or not, to view the different communities inside this network.

    Since the above layout does not help to visualize how the business are spacially located we have decided to display them with a map background.     Network of elite users

    Here we are focusing on the established links between users. As mentionned above you are only focusing on elite users from different years that are influent (at least 10 friends) and active (they have at least written 50 reviews). We have decided to draw this network in order pinpoint the different users communities.
    Blue nodes represent the elite users we are considering and the red ones are regular users. When on "With Communities" mode, you can easily distinguish the communities by color.
